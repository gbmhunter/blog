## Resistor E Series Finder

Added an [interactive E series resistor finder to the Resistors page](/electronics/components/resistors/#the-e-series). Enter a desired resistance and the widget shows the closest preferred value (and the closest equal-or-lower / equal-or-higher value) in each of the E6, E12, E24, E48, E96 and E192 series, along with the percentage error for each. Inputs accept metric prefixes (e.g. `10.3k`, `2M2`, `470R`).
