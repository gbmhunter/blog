## Plastic Enclosures

### Examples

#### Hammond 1554 Series

The Hammond 1554 series is a family of watertight polycarbonate and ABS enclosures. They offer IP protection up to IP68.

<Image src={import('./_assets/hammond-1554ya2gycl-300x240mm-polycarbonate-enclosure.webp')} width="500px">A photo of the Hammond 1554YA2GYCL 300x240mm polycarbonate enclosure.[^hammond-1554-series-1554ya2gycl]</Image>

The ABS version is rated for indoor use only while the polycarbonate version is rated for outdoor use. The ABS version is available with a grey lid, whilst the polycarbonate version offers a grey, clear or smoked lid.

<Image src={import('./_assets/hammond-1554-lid-options.webp')} width="600px">The different lid options available for the Hammond 1554 series polycarbonate enclosures (only grey lids available for the ABS version).[^hammond-1554-series-product-page]</Image>

#### Fibox EK Series

The Fibox EK series is a family of polycarbonate enclosures. They range in size from 190x190x130 mm to 760x560x250 mm (for the single enclosures). 

<Image src={import('./_assets/fibox-ek-series-enclosures-photo.webp')} width="500px">A photo showing a range of products available from the Fibox EK series of enclosures.[^fibox-ek-series-product-page]</Image>

The family is split up into subfamilies which have the same width and length, but different heights (depths). Each subfamily is denoted with a letter(s) suffix after the EK. The subfamilies are:[^fibox-ek-series-product-page]

- EKH: 190 x 190 x 30/80/100/130/180 mm
- EKJ: 280 x 190 x 30/80/100/130/180 mm
- EKM: 280 x 190 x 30/80/100/130/180 mm
- EKO: 280 x 280 x 30/80/100/130/180 mm
- EKPE, EKPG: 380 x 280 x 30/80/100/130/150/180 mm
- EKPK, EKPL: 380 x 280 x 150/180/230 mm
- EKT: 560 x 280 x 30/100/130 mm
- EKU: 560 x 380 x 30/150/180 mm
- EKW: 760 x 560 x 100/150/250 mm

Mounting plates are available for many of the enclosure sizes.

<Image src={import('./_assets/fibox-ek-series-mounting-plate-offerings.webp')} width="500px">The screenshot from the Fibox website showing some of the mounting plate offerings for the EK series of enclosures.[^fibox-ek-series-accessories-product-page]</Image>

## Extruded Aluminium Enclosures

Extruded aluminium enclosures consist of one piece of box-section aluminium for the body, with two plastic/metal end caps and a optional bezel. These enclosures are really good for small/medium PCB projects. The extruded aluminium normally has guide rails on the insides so that PCBs can slide into it. With the case being made of metal, heatsinking is easy.

<Image src={import('./_assets/extruded-aluminium-enclosures-eurocard-pcb.jpg')} width="300px">Examples of extruded aluminium enclosures by Vero Technologies.</Image>

<Aside type="note">
Connectors/indicators typically have to come out from either end. Right-angled PCB mount connectors are the easy to use with this configuration (panel mount can also be used).
</Aside>

Hammond is a large manufacturer of this type of enclosure. See their page, [Extruded Aluminium Enclosures (Metal End Panels) (1455 Series)](http://www.hammondmfg.com/1455.htm).

**Some of the Compatible Connectors**

Mini-DIN (circular)

Mini-XLR (circular)

Molex Micro-Fit 3.0 Series (rectangular)
